About

The three cities pictured in the background are San Francisco, New Orleans, and Chattanooga, all of which I have ties to in different ways. I am a perpetual wanderer in search of home, a questioner of things-that-seem-true-but-may-not-be, and (most of the time) a humanist who hopes a better future. You can likely find me curled up in bed with a book or article about psychology, hunched over laptop poring over a piece of code, prepping a recipe I just found, slinging paint onto a canvas, or glued to a rock face figuring out how to get to the next hold. My current passion is exploring the intersections between tech, creativity, and health.
